Privacy Policy

This public site is a lightweight portfolio and enquiry experience. It is designed to collect as little information as possible while still letting visitors get in touch.

What this site stores

The site may store a small preference in your browser to remember whether you dismissed the cookie banner. No account system, analytics login, or hidden tracking stack is included in this static version.

Contact briefs

The contact form on the public version does not submit to a server. Instead, it opens your default email client with the project brief prefilled so you can review and send it yourself.

Chat assistant

The on-page assistant provides instant scripted answers for common questions. It does not send your messages to a third-party AI API in this GitHub Pages deployment.

Third-party services

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts. Your browser may contact Google to retrieve those assets. If you want a stricter privacy posture later, the fonts can be self-hosted.

Questions

If you need a stricter privacy setup, self-hosted fonts, a first-party form backend, or consent-managed analytics, that can be added in a production rebuild.
